Chicken pasta

What is Creamy Chicken Pasta

Chicken pasta is a simple pasta dish using chicken and cream

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of pasta
  • 1000 grams of chicken breast
  • 500 mililiters of cream
  • 1 Chicken stock cube
  1. boil 2 liters of water. Add some salt
  2. Whlist water is boilng fry 1000 grams of chicken until sealed, mixing in chicken stock cube
  3. Once water is boiling cook 500 grams of pasta until well done
  4. Add cream to chicken, turning down to low heat
  5. Once pasta is cooked add to chicken and cream sauce, ready to serve
