Roast Beef

Roast Beef is a classic and easy to preper way of enjoying beef

  • 1.5 KG of topside beef
  • 2 Medium onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 sticks of Celery
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 1 bunch of mixed herbs such as rosemary, thyme, bay and sage
  • Olive Oil
  1. Remove beef from fridge 30 minutes before cooking so it can come to room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 240 C
  3. Wash and chop vegtables, break garlic into cloves
  4. Pile all the vegtables, herbs and garlic in the middle of a large roasting tray and drizzle with Oil. Place the beef on top of the vegtables.
  5. Place the tray in the oven turning the temperature down to 200 C and cook for 1 hour for medium beef. For medium rare, take it out 5 to 10 minutes earlier, for well done take it out 10 to 15 minutes later.
  6. If your doing roast potatoes put them in the oven for the last 45 miniutes
  7. Baste the beef half way through cooking and if the veg looks dry spash water on them to stop them from burning
  8. When the beef is cooked to your tatse take it out and let it rest for 15 minutes, covering in tin foil.
