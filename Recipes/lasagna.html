Lasagna

What is Lasagna

Lasgana is a oven cooked pasta dish featuring lots of beef mince, tomato and cheese

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Onion, Garlic, carrot and celery
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • milk
  • cheese
  • Lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Make a Bolognese sauce, do this by cooking mince in a pan and add tomato, garlic, onion, carrot, celery and redwine to the pan.
  2. Make Bechamel sauce by melting butter in a pan over medium heat, add flour. cook stiring for one or two minutes until mixture bubbles gradually stiring in milk.Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to medium. Cook, stirring, for 4 to 5 minutes or until mixture thickens.
  3. Make the base by smearing some bolongese sauce on the first sheet
  4. To make layer one place a lasanage sheet over the base and top this layer with bologense sauce and white sauce
  5. lay out more lasagna sheets, then top with more meat sauce and more white sauce
  6. to make the top cover with lasagna sheets, pour over remaining white sauce then sprinkle with cheese.
  7. Bake lasanage in a oven for 40 miniutes at 180C
